Frequently Asked Questions

Are the QR codes really free? ▼
Yes! SmartGen provides free QR code generation for all users. You can create unlimited QR codes without any hidden costs or premium upgrades required.
Do the generated QR codes expire? ▼
Static QR codes never expire—they're yours forever. Dynamic QR codes also don't expire; you can edit their destination links anytime without changing the code itself.
Can I use these QR codes for commercial purposes? ▼
Absolutely! You can use SmartGen QR codes for any purpose—personal, commercial, or business. There are no restrictions on how you use them.
What's the difference between static and dynamic QR codes? ▼
Static QR codes are fixed and cannot be edited after creation. Dynamic QR codes allow you to update the destination link anytime without regenerating the code, and they provide scan tracking analytics.
Can I upload my own logo to the QR code? ▼
Yes! SmartGen allows you to upload custom logos that will be placed in the center of your QR code. The logo is automatically optimized to maintain QR code scannability.
What file formats can I download? ▼
You can download your QR codes in PNG format for digital use and SVG format for scalable, print-ready versions. Both formats maintain high quality.
Is there a limit to how many QR codes I can create? ▼
No limit! Create as many QR codes as you need. SmartGen is designed to support unlimited QR code generation.
Is my data secure and private? ▼
Yes, SmartGen is 100% secure and privacy-focused. We don't track your personal information or store your QR code data on our servers without your consent.
